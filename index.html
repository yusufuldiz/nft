FAQ

WHAT IS AN NFT?

NFT stands for “Non-Fungible Token“. Sounds technical but basically it's a digital proof of ownership. Every NFT represents a unique digital item that users can buy, own and trade. NFTs can represent items such as photos, videos, audio, and other types of digital files. Some can also offer additional utility like exclusive access to websites or events.

WHERE DOES GO MY NFT AFTER I PURCHASE IT?

You will have to set up a crypto wallet like MetaMask and we will transfer the NFT to your wallet.

HOW CAN I GET MY VENLY WALLET AT OPENSEA?

You can easily import a wallet on another blockchain with Venly. This can be useful if you sent assets on one chain by mistake to a wallet on another chain. Or to have the same wallet address on multiple chains. To do this, go to your desired blockchain, click on the "import wallet" icon and select "From Matic" / "From Binance SC" / "From Ethereum". Sign it with your PIN and done! The assets in your wallet on that specific chain will be visible in your wallet..

WHAT CAN I DO WITH MY NFT?
